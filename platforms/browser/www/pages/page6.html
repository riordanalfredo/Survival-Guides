Survival Apps & Website

Are you lost?

Do you need directions to anywhere in Melbourne?
  • PTV Apps Faster access to Victoria public transport information on the go.
  • Tram Tracker App Includes predicted tram arrival times & disruption updates
  • 13Cabs App Taxi Booking Service
  • Uber App Cheaper option than taxi for a ride.
  • Google Maps Find local businesses, view maps and get driving directions.

Are you hungry?

Need references about restaurants/cafe or food delivery?
  • Uber Eats App Get the food you want, from the Melbourne restaurants you love, delivered at Uber speed.
  • Foodora App Premium food delivery to your doorstep from a curated choice of local restaurants
  • Zomato App View restaurant Menus, Photos, Ratings and User Reviews
  • Eatnow App Order food and pick up or get home delivery
  • MenuLog App Order food and pick up or get home delivery

Are you searching for accommodation around Victoria?

  • Airbnb App The Worldwide alternative to hotels and unique accommodations
  • Realestate.com.au Find the latest homes for sale, property news & real estate data
  • Koskosan.com.au offers listings for all types of residential properties, share accommodation, long term accommodation and long term holiday rentals.

Monashian Must-Have Apps

  • My.monash App Provides convenient access to student email, timetable, dates, and Moodle
  • Lost On Campus by StudentVIP provides campus maps, locations, photos carpark, bus stop, sportsfield, toilet, coffee shop and others.
