Welcome!

First of all, Perhimpunan Pelajar Indonesia di Australia (PPIA) Monash would like to welcome you to Melbourne and welcome to Monash University / Monash College!

If this is your first year in Melbourne, want to know more about Melbourne and how to survive your first year in Melbourne, you are reading the right book! We understand the life-changing experience of transiting to Melbourne, so we try our best to list helpful survival guides, from food to accommodation, events to road-trips and many more! We hope this would ease your adaptation to your new life transition in Melbourne.

We wish you all the best and looking forward to seeing you at our next events!
